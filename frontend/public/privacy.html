Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED: JULY 11, 2026

Atrium (the "Platform," "we," "us," or "our") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, use, encrypt, store, and process your data when you interact with the Atrium Telegram Bot, Chrome Extension, and Companion Web Application.

1. Information We Collect & Ingest

We only collect data that you explicitly submit or configure to sync with Atrium:

  • Media and Files: Images (screenshots, graphs, photos), voice notes (audio clips, memos), and document files (PDFs) uploaded to our Telegram Bot or Web App for text extraction, metadata parsing, and indexing.
  • Web Content: Tab URLs, page titles, and body texts clipped and submitted by you using the Atrium browser extension.
  • Credentials: Google Drive OAuth refresh tokens, which are encrypted immediately and used strictly to back up your notes to your Google Drive account.
  • Temporary Timezone Location: When configuring your profile, you may optionally send a geolocation pin. This coordinates payload is processed **ephemerally** to auto-detect your timezone offset and is **never stored permanently** in our database.
  • Operational Logs: We may temporarily collect IP addresses, request metadata, device information, and server logs for security, abuse prevention, debugging, and operational purposes.
  • Error Tracking & Diagnostics: We may use diagnostic and error monitoring services such as Sentry to record error logs, application performance data, and diagnostics to resolve software bugs and system failures.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Authenticate your account and verify your identity;
  • Provide search, organization, and AI-powered features;
  • Synchronize content across supported integrations (such as Google Drive);
  • Generate summaries, quizzes, tags, and embeddings;
  • Process data exports and backups you request;
  • Maintain the security, reliability, and operation of the Service; and
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

Atrium may use cookies or browser storage (such as localStorage) to maintain authentication sessions, remember preferences, and improve user experience.

4. Cryptographic Security & Data Storage

Atrium employs encryption, access controls, rate limiting, system monitoring, and secure communication protocols designed to protect your data:

  • At-Rest Encryption: Sensitive data is protected using industry-standard encryption and access controls where appropriate.
  • Infrastructure Isolation: Our hosted database and caching layers employ access controls, encryption, and hashing to secure sensitive data fields, reducing the risk and impact of unauthorized access.
  • In-Transit Security: All network traffic between your browser, browser extension, Telegram Webhook, and backend servers is encrypted using Secure Sockets Layer/Transport Layer Security (HTTPS/TLS).

5. Downstream AI Processing & Sub-Processors

To summarize, tag, and generate vector embeddings for your saved content, Atrium routes plaintext data to third-party AI interfaces. We transmit only the minimum information reasonably necessary to perform the AI feature you requested. These third-party processors include: Google Gemini API, Groq API, and NVIDIA NIM.

⚠️ AI Processing Disclaimer

Certain AI providers may retain prompts or outputs according to their own terms and policies. Please review the privacy policies of those providers before submitting highly sensitive information.

7. Data Portability & Deletion

You retain full sovereignty and ownership over your data:

  • Account Deletion: When you delete your account (which triggers a DELETE /api/me request), Atrium deletes your account data from active systems. Residual copies may temporarily persist in operational backups until those backups expire or are overwritten.
  • Data Portability: You can export all saved items, tags, and AI summaries using the export features available within your dashboard settings.
  • Data Retention Policy: We retain your personal data and content only for as long as your account remains active. Ephemeral background task payloads in our queues are routinely removed after processing. We do not maintain long-term offline database backups; point-in-time recovery logs are retained only for a limited operational period.

8. Third-Party Services

Atrium integrates with several independent third-party services to function (such as Telegram, Google Drive, Groq, and NVIDIA). Atrium does not control or accept responsibility for the independent privacy practices of third-party providers.

9. Security Disclaimer & Safeguards

We implement administrative, physical, and technical safeguards designed to protect the integrity of your data. However, you acknowledge that no transmission over the internet or cloud infrastructure is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee that unauthorized access, leaks, or system hacks will never occur, and your use of the Service is at your own risk.

10. Security Reports

If you believe you have discovered a security vulnerability, please contact us at atriumhelp@outlook.com. Please do not publicly disclose vulnerabilities until we have had a reasonable opportunity to investigate and address them.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own by our service providers (including the United States). Where applicable, we take reasonable steps to ensure such transfers comply with applicable law.

12. Territorial Scope & GDPR

Atrium is operated from India. If applicable law (including GDPR or similar privacy laws) applies to your use of the Service, we will process personal data in accordance with those applicable legal requirements.

13. Privacy Inquiries

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, your data deletion rights, data exports, or security concerns, contact us at atriumhelp@outlook.com.
